<div class="lang-en">
Gemini is Google's state-of-the-art multimodal assistant. Capable of analyzing massive datasets, understanding audio transcripts, and reading long hours of video natively, Gemini represents a massive step forward in AI utility.

### Key Features
- **1M+ Context Window**: Standard Gemini 1.5 Pro models support up to 2 million tokens of context, letting you upload entire codebases or feature-length movies.
- **Native Multimodal Parsing**: Handles voice notes, images, PDFs, and video streams in a single query.
- **Deep Google Integration**: Integrates directly with Docs, Drive, Gmail, Maps, and YouTube.

### How to Get Started
1. Access [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com) using your Google account.
2. Click the '+' button to upload a large PDF or video file.
3. Prompt Gemini to extract key insights, translate transcripts, or write code based on the files.
